Design Ethnography

This project aimed to understand Ethnographic Design Approach in the topic of "The main meal from shopping to dish" which detects human natural behavior by taking acount into the four principals

:
  1. Natural Behavior:
    2. &nbps;Through fieldwork, you will be able to see their honest behavior.
  2. Holism&nbps;Instead of looking into specific aspects such as individual behavior, an overall situation (E.g., social influence, culture, and traditional) and people’s perspective as a whole would enhance the understanding of particular behavior.
  3. Descriptive&nbps;It is important to exclude any interpretations and assumptions during ethnography to comprehend how people actually behave.
  4. Member’s Point-of-View&nbps;thnography aims to obtain people’s insights and values which connect to the meanings of behind their behavior.

Storyboard

Throughout the experience of using my idea, Storyboard helps me understand the user's storyline. In this project, I focused on entertaining people while being stuck in traffic.

The storyboard, therefore, shows how do the users feel about the experience and what are the steps they would take in order to complete the idea.

Whack Pack Cards

Whack Pack Cards helps to discover another way of creative thinking by forcing making a connection between the idea and the cards. When I evaluated my idea, I picked up one of the cards "Exaggerate" which implies how the idea can expand the potential.

This card made me step back to the ideation phase. Instead of having a simple immersive audiobook, I added a game function that people can choose a story's path by voting among the members.
