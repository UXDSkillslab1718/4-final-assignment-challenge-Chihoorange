Design & Creativity

The main purpose of this project was to visualize ideas by obtaining a variety of ideation techniques, such as mindmap, storyboard, and whack pack card evaluation.

See more details >>.

Mindmap

This technique starts by drawing up the knowledge you already know by categorizing into sections in order to comprehend a broad view of your target field.

Storyboard

This technique helps to understand a user's point of view. By making a storyline, this allows picturing what the user would think and feel so that you can cover their needs and steps they need to take account into.

Whack Ppack Card Evaluation

When your idea comes to an evaluation step, this technique implies another way of thinking. By using these cards, it would help you to see how you could improve your idea.

Research for Design

The ethnographic design approach applied in this project to detect users' needs. Through interviews, observation, and reading research, I was allowed to create two personas so that I can convey insightful information to designers.

See more details >>.

Ethnoglaphic Interview

This design approach focuses on users' natural behavior. Thus, the interview was semi-structured in order to make my participants feel not interrogated because that would influence their naturalistic point of view.

Observation

Instead of hearing their point of view, observation would help understand their natural bahvior.

Reading Activity

Reading Activity might be a good start for looking at your users; need and context. Any reading sources including news, blogs, magazines, and scientific literature are helpful to identify the users' insights.
